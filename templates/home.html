Synthetic Dataset Evaluation Study

Welcome to Our Research Study

Thank you for participating in our synthetic dataset evaluation study. Your contribution will help us better understand how humans recognize the identities, which can inspire the future synthetic dataset generation studies to better solve the privacy issue.

What You'll Be Doing

In this study, you will be shown 100 sets of face images and asked to answer if these images are from the same identity.

Before You Begin

  • We'll show you several example cases to help you understand the task
  • Take your time to observe the facial features in each image
  • There are no right or wrong answers - we value your honest perception
  • After you finish your survey, do not click the submit button and raise your hand to indicate the completion.
  • Your responses will be kept anonymous and used for research purposes only
